About Hack Star

At Hack Star, we're igniting the space hackathon movement with our upcoming events in the Chicagoland area and India. By partnering with Hack Club, we aim to fuel young minds' passion for space exploration and technology. Join us on a journey to innovate and push the boundaries of the known.
